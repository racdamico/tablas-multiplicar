🏡 What are villages like?

People live together in places called settlements. A settlement has houses, streets and buildings. The number of people who live there is the population.

A village is a small settlement with a small population. In the centre there is a main square. The town hall and the church are there. Streets are short and narrow. Buildings are low. There are few services.

3 types: 🏔️ Tourist villages — mountains or sea, hotels and restaurants. 🏭 Industrial villages — factories and farms, more services. 🌾 Small villages — shrink because young people leave to work in cities.

🔑 Key Words
village = pueblo
population = población
main square = plaza mayor
town hall = ayuntamiento
settlement = núcleo de población
narrow = estrecho

🏙️ What are towns and cities like?

Towns and cities are large settlements with a large population. Cities have hospitals, universities, offices, theatres and cinemas.

3 parts: 🏛️ Historic centre — old, narrow streets, monuments. 🏢 Modern district — wide streets, tall buildings. 🏘️ Suburbs — houses, shopping centres, far from centre.

✅ Advantages: services, jobs, different cultures. ❌ Disadvantages: traffic jams, pollution, loneliness. 💡 Solutions: public transport, bicycles, support services.

🔑 Key Words
historic centre = centro histórico
modern district = barrio moderno
suburb = barrio periférico
traffic jam = atasco
pollution = contaminación
zero-emission = cero emisiones

🏛️ What is the local council?

Every village, town or city is a municipality. It has a local council — the government that represents all the people.

The workers are councillors. The head is the mayor. They work in the town hall.

Be a responsible citizen: 🗳️ Vote every 4 years. 🤝 Join local associations. 📋 Respect the laws — no litter, road safety, clean after your dog! 🐕

🔑 Key Words

local council = ayuntamiento
mayor = alcalde/alcaldesa
councillor = concejal

town hall = casa consistorial

🚌 What are local services?

The local council organises local services for all citizens. We must look after and respect them!

4 main services: 👮 Police & Fire — safety and rescues. 🚌 Transport — buses and trains. 🎨 Cultural & Leisure — museums, libraries, sports. 🗑️ Waste & Sanitation — rubbish, clean city, clean water.

💰 What are municipal taxes?

The local council needs money to pay for local services. This money comes from taxes — money citizens must pay to the government.

🏠 Property tax — own a house → pays for streets, sewers, parks. 🚗 Road tax — own a car → pays for roads and traffic signs.

Everyone uses local services, so everyone helps pay with taxes. They help us improve our community! 🤝

🔑 Key Words
tax / taxes = impuesto/s
property tax = IBI
road tax = impuesto vehículos
sewer = alcantarilla
citizen = ciudadano
traffic sign = señal de tráfico
